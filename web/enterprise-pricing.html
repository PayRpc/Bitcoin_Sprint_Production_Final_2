Frequently Asked Questions

How does Bitcoin Sprint compare to IPFS pinning services?
Unlike basic IPFS pinning services that only store files, Bitcoin Sprint provides cryptographic proof-of-storage with SHA-256 hashing and Merkle tree verification. We offer real-time validation, multi-protocol support, and enterprise-grade security features that traditional pinning services lack.
What makes your service better than Filecoin?
While Filecoin focuses on storage mining incentives, Bitcoin Sprint provides superior validation technology with faster response times (<2s vs hours), unified API for multiple protocols, AI-powered analytics, and enterprise features like SLA monitoring and webhook integrations.
Do you support custom protocols?
Yes! Our plugin-based architecture supports custom protocols. Enterprise customers can integrate their proprietary storage solutions with our validation engine for comprehensive proof-of-storage capabilities.
What security measures are in place?
We implement end-to-end encryption, API key authentication, rate limiting, circuit breakers, audit trails, and SOC 2 Type II compliance. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with regular security audits and penetration testing.
Can I get a white-label solution?
Absolutely! Our Enterprise plan includes white-label options where you can customize the interface, branding, and domain. We also offer on-premise deployment for maximum control and compliance.
